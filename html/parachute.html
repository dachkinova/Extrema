The possibility of flying with a hot-air balloon in Bulgaria and Romania is a relatively new tourist attraction. A new tourism product has been created, which will contribute for promoting of the image of cross-border region Romania-Bulgaria as a tourist destination for adventure tourism combined with many other tourism opportunities.
